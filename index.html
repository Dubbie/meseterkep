The team of MeseTérKép was founded in January 2017 with the aim of holding tales-based creative workshops for children who crave for not every-day adventures. They operate in Pécs and the surrounding cities.

The creators of the concept are three young women working in different fields of education. Vilja is a big fan of children's museums, Kitti's a literature teacher and Réka is an art therapist. The project was created by combining the knowledge and ambition of the three.
