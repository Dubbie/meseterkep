Book Workshop!

Once every month we provide a shared experience for parent and baby, where we can discover the world of different colors, shapes and materials. During the session, we use edible paint, so we can be covered from head to toe!

Every week we explore a new world of tales on the basis of our monthly changing themes. Whether we are astronauts, car mechanics or deep sea archaeologists that week, the aim is to free your imagination, exploring literature, art techniques, and new tools.
